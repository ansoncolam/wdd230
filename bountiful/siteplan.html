Bountiful Foods Site Plan

Anson Lam

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a channel for online customers to order fresh fruit products from local farmers and suppliers in a timely and easy way.

Target Audience

Our target audience are those people who seek for a healthy life style or want to try something new in their lives.

Personas

Persona 1 - Aaron Anderson is a 30-year-old single adult. He is currenlty working as an accountant in an accounting firm. Busy work life makes him feel sick sometimes; therefore, he always want to find some ways to maintain his health both mentally and physically as much as he can.

Persona 2 - Betty Brand is a yound mum with 2 children (age 5 & age 7). She takes care of her family and every house work at home every day while her husband is working abroad lately. She pays extra attention on what food to provide to her 2 young childfren; as she wants her children to grow up healthily with the best nutrient.
